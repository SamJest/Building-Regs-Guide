# Traffic Upgrade Summary

Date: 2026-04-27

## What Changed

- Created the Search Console opportunity report at `reports/search-console-opportunities.md`.
- Improved metadata generation for local project pages, council pages, local-search pages, tool pages and selected project/rule combinations.
- Added stricter metadata validation for missing titles/metas, long titles/metas, duplicate titles, repeated bland templates and unexpected `noindex`.
- Upgraded local project pages with answer-first “You may need planning permission if” and “Usually simpler if” cards.
- Added a “Popular planning questions in this area” module to local project pages, linking to relevant council, local rule, comparison and tool pages.
- Moved official source blocks higher on local project pages so verification links appear closer to the answer.
- Strengthened trust blocks with clearer “rules vary by location” and official-source verification notices.
- Made curated local-search pages indexable and included them in the sitemap because Search Console already shows demand for those routes.
- Added a focused set of new local-search/supporting pages from GSC demand rather than generating a large new page family.

## Templates Improved

- Local project pages generated by `generators/site_core.py`
- Project-specific rule pages generated by `generators/project_scenario_pages.py`
- Curated local-search pages generated by `generators/local_search_pages.py`
- Tool pages generated by `generators/planning_tools.py`
- Shared metadata builders in `components/seo.py`
- Trust and official-source components in `components/trust_framework.py` and `components/official_sources.py`

## Page Types Targeted

- HMO and Article 4 local-search pages
- Council/local-search pages with early impressions
- Porch pages, especially Cardiff/Wales-style searches
- Dropped-kerb pages where planning vs highway approval matters
- Conservation-area pages
- Extension and alteration-plan support pages
- Tool pages and rule self-check tools

## Search Console Opportunities Addressed

- HMO/Article 4 queries with high rankings and zero clicks now have stronger HMO + Article 4 + permitted-development title wording.
- Local-search pages for Newham, Westminster, Tamworth, Leicestershire, Charnwood and similar routes are now indexable and listed in the local-search sitemap.
- Cardiff porch pages now produce more direct porch planning-permission metadata and answer-first sections.
- New support pages were added for:
  - industrial alteration plans Bromsgrove
  - domestic alteration plans Hagley
  - St Albans planning permitted development
  - permitted development Cambridge
  - extensions Merton Park
  - rear extension Merton Park
  - extension planning permission Poole
  - extensions Uttlesford
  - side extensions South Hams

## Expected Impact

- Higher CTR on pages already ranking positions 5-15 because titles and snippets now match local/project intent more directly.
- Better indexing quality for curated local-search pages that were previously generated but de-emphasised.
- Stronger engagement from above-the-fold answers, clearer next checks and contextual tool CTAs.
- Better internal link equity into underperforming but relevant local, council and rule pages.
- Clearer trust signals without overstating legal certainty.
