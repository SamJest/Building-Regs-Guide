# Batch 5 source update notes

Source handling remains strict:

- Building regulations approval is different from planning permission. Pages that compare the two must say both may be needed.
- Competent person schemes only remove the need for the homeowner to apply directly for covered work by registered scheme members. They are not a general building-regulations exemption.
- Approved Documents are statutory guidance on ways to meet building regulations, not the whole legal regime.
- Approved Document L 2026 was published on 24 March 2026 and earlier versions can still apply to buildings under previous standards.
- Future Homes and Buildings Standards Circular 01/2026 introduced updated statutory guidance for Approved Document L and Approved Document F.
- Higher-risk building work must route to BSR-specific guidance; ordinary domestic route logic must not be applied where HRB thresholds are triggered.
