# Approved Document G: Sanitation, hot water safety and water efficiency

Approved Document G is the practical guidance route for **sanitary conveniences, washing facilities, hot water safety, water efficiency and wholesome water**. This page should help a homeowner understand when this Part is likely to matter, what evidence to keep, and which project pages to read next.

This is not a substitute for a designer, structural engineer, registered installer, building control body or the official Approved Document. Codex should render this as a plain-English hub with prominent links to the official source and to project-specific checks.

## When this Part commonly appears

- new bathrooms
- ensuites
- kitchens
- utility rooms
- extensions with new plumbing
- new dwellings

## What the homeowner normally needs to prove

- hot water safety details
- water efficiency calculation where required
- sanitary layout
- plumbing/commissioning certificates

## Common red flags

- unvented hot water cylinders
- new bathrooms without ventilation
- layout reducing accessible WC provision
- high water-use fittings in new dwellings
