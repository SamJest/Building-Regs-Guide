# Approved Document D: Toxic substances

Approved Document D is the practical guidance route for **limiting risks from toxic substances, especially cavity insulation formaldehyde-related issues**. This page should help a homeowner understand when this Part is likely to matter, what evidence to keep, and which project pages to read next.

This is not a substitute for a designer, structural engineer, registered installer, building control body or the official Approved Document. Codex should render this as a plain-English hub with prominent links to the official source and to project-specific checks.

## When this Part commonly appears

- cavity wall insulation
- insulation upgrades
- retrofit energy work

## What the homeowner normally needs to prove

- product datasheets
- installer certificate
- specification confirming suitable materials

## Common red flags

- unknown insulation products
- unverified installer claims
- older cavity fill records missing

## Related Approved Documents

This Part often overlaps with Part C, Part F, Part L. The page must not present any Part in isolation where a normal project usually crosses several requirements.
