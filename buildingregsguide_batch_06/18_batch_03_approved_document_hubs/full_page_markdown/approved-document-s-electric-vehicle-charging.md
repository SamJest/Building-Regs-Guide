# Approved Document S: Electric vehicle charging infrastructure

Approved Document S is the practical guidance route for **EV charge point and cable-route provision for new residential and certain other building work**. This page should help a homeowner understand when this Part is likely to matter, what evidence to keep, and which project pages to read next.

This is not a substitute for a designer, structural engineer, registered installer, building control body or the official Approved Document. Codex should render this as a plain-English hub with prominent links to the official source and to project-specific checks.

## When this Part commonly appears

- new dwellings
- major residential renovations
- new parking provision
- some non-residential works

## What the homeowner normally needs to prove

- charge point/cable route design
- electrical load assessment
- Part P/EIC paperwork
- parking bay plan

## Common red flags

- new homes with parking
- major renovation with parking
- consumer unit capacity concerns
- leasehold/communal parking constraints

## Related Approved Documents

This Part often overlaps with Part P, Part L. The page must not present any Part in isolation where a normal project usually crosses several requirements.
