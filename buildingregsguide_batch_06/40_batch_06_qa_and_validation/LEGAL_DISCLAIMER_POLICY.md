# Legal and compliance wording policy

Use plain warnings, not panic wording.

Preferred wording:

- `This is a practical guide, not formal approval.`
- `Check with your building control body, registered installer or qualified professional before starting work.`
- `The likely route depends on your exact property, design, timing and local/building-control requirements.`
- `Keep certificates and completion paperwork because buyers, solicitors and insurers may ask for evidence later.`

Avoid:

- `You are approved.`
- `This confirms your work complies.`
- `You do not need building control.`
- `Guaranteed route.`
- `Legal certificate.`
