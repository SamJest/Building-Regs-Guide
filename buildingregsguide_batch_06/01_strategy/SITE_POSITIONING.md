# BuildingRegsGuide Site Positioning

## Core job

UKPlanningGuide answers: **Do I need planning permission?**

BuildingRegsGuide answers: **Do I need building regulations approval, which building-control route fits, and what proof/certificates should I keep?**

## Best initial audience

Homeowners in England doing small-to-medium domestic work:

- extensions
- loft conversions
- garage conversions
- structural alterations
- garden rooms and outbuildings
- windows and doors
- electrical work
- boilers/heating/heat pumps
- bathrooms/kitchens/drainage
- missing certificates before sale/remortgage

## Differentiation

Most pages online explain building regulations in broad terms. This site should be more useful by turning each topic into:

1. a direct answer;
2. a route decision;
3. a document/evidence checklist;
4. a printable project file;
5. a handoff to planning only where planning still matters.

## Tone

Plain English. Practical. Official-source backed. Avoid pretending to provide design, engineering or legal sign-off.

## First-site constraint

England-first. Add Wales and Scotland as source-aware guardrail pages. Do not flatten all UK nations into one answer.
