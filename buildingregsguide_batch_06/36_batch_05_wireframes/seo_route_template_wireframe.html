Building regulations question

Do I need building regulations for a loft conversion?

Plain-English answer

Most loft conversions need building regulations approval because structure, fire safety, stairs, insulation and electrics are usually affected.

What usually triggers approval

  • Structural floor changes
  • New stairs
  • Fire safety changes
